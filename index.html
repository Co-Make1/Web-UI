Co Make

"Join hands with your community to make a difference."

What is CoMake?

CoMake is an app that allows active community members to choose what tasks they would like the local gov. to focus on in their region.

Who is this app for?

This app is for all members of a community. So long as you have an idea as to what you want done, there is no limitation as to who can use the app.

How does it work?

The concept of Co-Make is simple: Vote for a task or issue and the list will be available for gov officials to look over. The highest voted tasks will most likely get priority.
